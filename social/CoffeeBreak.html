Coffee Breaks

All days between 10:15 and 11:00, and between 15:30 and 16:15

    Spain is known for its good weather. And September is a month that usually has mild, warm temperatures and many hours of sun shine. That is why we have decided that coffee breaks at #icsme18 will be outside in a beautiful terrace.

    Coffee breaks will be in the beautiful terrace of Fábula, just opposite to the Círculo de Bellas Artes. The terrace has a magnificent, protected garden where conference participants will enjoy coffee and pastries while socializing. As we will have to go down and cross the street, we have scheduled for longer coffee breaks as usual (45 minutes instead of the usual 30 minutes), but we think it is worth the change. And, no worries -- if it rains, we have a plan B.
