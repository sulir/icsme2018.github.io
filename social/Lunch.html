Lunches

All days between 12:30 and 14:00

    Spain is known for its good taste and variety of food. ICSME 2018 could not be different and we have organized some special arrangements so that participants enjoy not only the program, but good food in a nice surrounding and (hopefully) the good weather.

    Lunch will be served in the Las Bóvedas de Cibeles Restaurant, crossing Alcaláa street. Instead of a standing cocktail, we have chosen a typical Spanish restaurant with a special taste, where the menú del día can be appreciated, which includes two courses and dessert. Conference participants may accompany their meal with local wine and beer, if desired (and at no cost).
